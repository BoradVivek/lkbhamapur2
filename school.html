Welcome to SMT Labhuben Kanjibhai Babariya Secondary and Higher Secondary School Hamapur

About Us

Established with a vision to nurture young minds, SMT Labhuben Kanjibhai Babariya Secondary and Higher Secondary School Hamapur is dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development for students from secondary to higher secondary levels.

Our mission is to foster an environment that inspires learning, creativity, discipline, and social responsibility, preparing students for a promising future.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes:

  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Mathematics
  • Commerce and Accountancy
  • Languages: English, Hindi, Gujarati
  • Computer Science & Information Technology
  • Arts and Humanities

Our teachers are committed to inspiring excellence and critical thinking in all students.

Our teachers are also provides old exam question papers and sample exam papers to the
students to prepare and do practice for exam to passout from exam easily.

Also our teachers take monthly test to know that which student have to do more hard work
for each subject.

We also provide perodical test to our student to make them strong and for prepare for exam.
