Exam System

We conduct regular evaluations and exams to ensure academic excellence. The academic year is divided into two terms:

  • First Term: Periodical tests, unit tests, and a half-yearly exam (April to October)
  • Second Term: Periodical tests and final exam (November to March)

We ensure that students are well-prepared and supported to pass exams successfully.
